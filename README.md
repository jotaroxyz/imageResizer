# Image Resizer

A simple open-source image resizing app, providing a fast, lightweight and offline-first solution for resizing your images right from your desktop.

Refer to [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for contribution guidelines and to see our Contributor License Agreement.\
Refer to [NOTICE.md](./NOTICE.md) for additional information and legal notices.

</div>

## Supported formats
Works seamlessly with standard image formats (PNG, JPEG, etc.).\
(We may plan on expanding it later)

## 💾 Installation & Download
It's as easy as it sounds!
1. Go to the newest [release](https://github.com/jotaroxyz/imageResizer/releases/latest)
2. Download the package matching your operating system.
3. Launch the app and and that's it!
